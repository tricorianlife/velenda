What Velenda unifies

One operating layer for the work that runs a modern pharma manufacturer.

Velenda collapses the three motions that decide margin into a single coordinated layer. Less stack. Fewer handoffs. One source of truth, fed by the database underneath.

Formulation

Bring discovery and reformulation into the same environment as your supplier intelligence. Composition decisions made with live visibility into materials, suppliers and downstream cost, before specs lock and change orders get expensive.

Procurement

Move beyond the spreadsheet, the supplier portal and the inbox. Source against a global, qualified materials base with quality, lead time and commercial terms in one view. Today this runs as our service. Soon it runs inside your workflow.

Manufacturing

Production, quality and supply orchestrated against the same data your buyers and formulators use. Fewer disconnected systems, fewer reconciliations, faster response when a material, a batch or a supplier moves.
